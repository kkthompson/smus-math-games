How to Play
The Game
Answer 10 multiplication questions before the timer runs out on each one. Tap the correct answer from the choices on screen. Your Blitz Score combines how many you get right with how fast you answer.
⚡ Easy
6 answer choices. Wrong answers are other products from the same table — if you know your table, you'll spot the right one fast.
⚡⚡ Medium
6 answer choices. Wrong answers now include numbers very close to the right answer (like 34 instead of 35), so you need to be more precise.
⚡⚡⚡ Hard
6 answer choices with tricky near-miss distractors from neighbouring tables. Some questions are reversed — you'll see "? × 5 = 35" and need to find the missing number!
💀 Extra Hard
9 answer choices instead of 6! The same tricky distractors as Hard, plus reversed questions. Only for the bravest mathematicians.
🎲 Mix it up!
Questions come from any times table between 2 and 12 — great for practising everything at once.
